Frequently Asked Question

How do I book an appointment?

To book an appointment, you can visit our "Book Appointment" page, select your preferred doctor, choose a convenient time slot, and complete the booking process by filling out the necessary details.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Please bring your ID, insurance card (if applicable), and any relevant medical records or test results. If you are a new patient, please arrive 15 minutes early to complete the registration process.

Can I reschedule or cancel my appointment?

Yes, you can reschedule or cancel your appointment through our website or by contacting our office directly. Please notify us at least 24 hours in advance to avoid any cancellation fees.

Do you accept walk-in patients?

We do accept walk-in patients, but availability is limited and wait times may be longer. To ensure you are seen promptly, we recommend booking an appointment in advance.

What are your office hours?

Our office hours are Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM. We are closed on weekends and public holidays. For emergencies, please visit the nearest hospital.
